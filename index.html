About Iceland

The Land of Fire and Ice

The Land of Fire and Ice. Iceland is a unique Nordic island, known for its stunning natural landscapes combining active volcanoes, majestic glaciers, erupting geysers, and soothing hot springs. Located in the North Atlantic, this magical land offers natural spectacles such as the Northern Lights in winter and the Midnight Sun in summer.
In addition to its natural beauty, Iceland is a perfect destination for adventure lovers, with activities such as hiking in lava fields, exploring ice caves, and relaxing in the famous Blue Lagoon. Its culture, based on ancient Viking sagas and a modern, innovative society, makes this country a fascinating place to discover. An ideal destination to explore nature in its purest form!

What is there to do?

Volcano Horses

At Eldhestar we have developed our activities since 1986 to combine pleasure, comfort and adventure in a way respecting nature. Our farm is located in the heart of Iceland’s south-western countryside, only 30 minutes´ drive from Reykjavík.

Into the Glacier

Into the Glacier ofrece acceso al túnel de hielo más grande del mundo, ubicado en lo alto del glaciar Langjökull. Allí tendrá la oportunidad de explorar el glaciar y verlo desde dentro. En 2010, una visión audaz se hizo realidad: perforar un túnel en el glaciar para descubrir su fascinante "hielo azul".

Reykjavik Excursions

Reykjavik Excursions by Icelandia offers a wide variety of day tours, enabling travelers to visit and experience many of the most stunning and exotic places in Iceland: The Blue Lagoon, Northern Lights tours, the Golden Circle, national parks and the majestic Jökulsárlón glacier lagoon.

DIVE.IS

DIVE.IS is a dive center formed in 1997 and offered various diving courses. In early 2000's the company started offering both diving and snorkelingtours on daily basis to many sites close to the city. Silfra is definitely the dive site that helped DIVE.IS to grow so rapidly.
